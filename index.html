Profile

Meet Elusiyan Mathew Eluwole, also known as Royal Wolesax, a software engineer, songwriter, singer, and talented saxophonist. Dedicated to preaching the gospel of Jesus Christ through his music worldwide, he combines his saxophone skills with his passion for worship to create uplifting and inspiring music. His ministry transcends entertainment, spreading hope and love, and making a meaningful impact on society. Notably, Royal Wolesax has impacted the lives of children and orphans through his annual Undisputed Getup Charity Concert, initiated in 2012 out of his love for God and humanity.
